About Us


Comcare is a dedicated non-profit organization founded in 2024 by M-Tech Business Solutions (MBS) with the aim of providing comprehensive support to victims of the conflicts in Israel and Palestine. Our mission is to bring relief and hope to those who have endured unimaginable hardships and trauma due to the ongoing war. We believe that every individual, regardless of their background, deserves access to basic human necessities and the opportunity to rebuild their lives.





Our Vision



Our vision is to break the cycle of despair that often accompanies conflict, replacing it with a path to healing and empowerment. We understand that recovery from the trauma of war is not only about providing immediate aid but also about addressing the deeper, long-term challenges that victims face. This is why our approach is holistic, focusing on both urgent relief and sustainable solutions that restore hope, dignity, and a true sense of purpose to those we serve. We envision a world where no one is left behind, where the devastation of war does not define the future of those affected, but instead opens doors to new opportunities for recovery and renewal. At Comcare, we believe that victims of war are not merely survivors of their circumstances but are resilient individuals who, when given the right tools and resources, have the potential to rebuild their lives, their families and their communities at large.
