Ahmed's Journey to a New Beginning


Ahmed, a 35-year-old father of two, was forced to flee his home when his neighborhood was caught in the crossfire of escalating violence in Gaza. With nothing but the clothes on their backs, Ahmed and his family found themselves in a refugee camp, struggling to survive.

In need help, Ahmed reached out to Comcare and soon he and his family where provided with immediate necessities such as food and temporary shelter. But Comcare's support didn't stop there. Recognizing Ahmed's skills as a carpenter, they enrolled him in a vocational training program and helped him secure tools and materials to start his own small business.

With the steady income from his carpentry work, Ahmed was able to move his family into a more permanent home and send his children back to school. Today, Ahmed's business is thriving, and he is an active member of his community, often volunteering with Comcare to help other families in need. Ahmed's resilience and Comcare's unwavering support have given him and his family a new lease on life.





Fatima's Path to Healing and Empowerment


Fatima, a 28-year-old woman from the West Bank, lost her husband in a tragic bombing incident. Left alone to care for her child, Fatima was overwhelmed with grief and the enormous responsibility of providing for her daughter. She struggled to find work and was on the brink of despair.

She reached out to Comcare and was provided with emotional support through counseling sessions, which helped her cope with her loss and regain her strength. Recognizing her potential, Comcare also offered her a scholarship to attend a local university, where she pursued a degree in social work.

During her studies, Fatima interned at Comcare, where she discovered her passion for helping others. After graduating, she joined Comcare as a full-time employee, working to provide support and resources to other women and children affected by the conflict. Fatima's journey from a grieving widow to an empowered advocate for her community is a testament to her resilience and the life-changing impact of Comcare's support.





Samir's Return to Education and Hope


Samir, a 16-year-old boy from the outskirts of Jerusalem, had his education abruptly interrupted when his school was destroyed during a bombing. Displaced and living in a temporary shelter, Samir's dreams of becoming an engineer seemed out of reach.

Comcare intervened by providing Samir with educational support, including school supplies, books, and access to a temporary learning center established by the organization. Samir was also connected with a mentor who guided him through his studies and encouraged his passion for engineering.

Through Comcare's support, Samir was able to catch up on his lost school years and excel in his studies. He recently received a scholarship to attend a prestigious university where he is now pursuing a degree in civil engineering. Samir's determination, combined with the opportunities provided by Comcare, has set him on a path to achieving his dreams and contributing to the rebuilding of his community.
